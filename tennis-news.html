Cameron Norrie 'not happy' after throwing racket into crowd and hitting spectator

Cameron Norrie, the British tennis star, let his frustrations boil over during his defeat at the Auckland Classic, dramatically throwing his racket into the crowd. The incident came after a tough match, marking a rare display of anger from the usually composed player.

The 29-year-old got away with a warning from the umpire and went on to lose the Auckland Classic match 6-2, 6-3 to Facundo Diaz Acosta.

"I apologised very quickly and I want to apologise in general. I'm not happy with how I behaved," says the British number two.
